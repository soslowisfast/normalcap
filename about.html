About us

Normal Capital is an investment partnership focused on global equity opportunities. "Normal" in Chinese is "Zheng Chang". "Zheng" is interpreted as "do right things then do things right", while "Chang" represents "common sense and rationality". These principles form the core of the partnership's investment philosophy.

The partnership is managed by Patrick Ye. A father, student and investor, Patrick learnt about business and value investing through reading, his past mistakes, and past work experience as an investment banker, a tech worker, then a startup founder. He seeks tranquility in both life and investing.
